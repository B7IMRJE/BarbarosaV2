# BARBAROSA EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS PACKAGE

## Status

Optional

## Priority

Highly Recommended

## Purpose

The Emergency Preparedness Package is designed to help homeowners respond quickly during emergencies by providing immediate access to critical shutoff locations, equipment information, photos, and service company contact options.

The goal is to reduce property damage, improve communication with service providers, and help homeowners locate important controls during stressful situations.

---

# Homeowner Benefits

* Faster response during water leaks
* Faster response during gas emergencies
* Easier communication with service providers
* Reduced damage from delayed shutoffs
* Access to actual photos of equipment and controls
* Improved emergency preparedness

---

# Emergency Asset Photos

The following photos are highly recommended:

## Water System

* Main Water Shutoff
* Water Meter
* Pressure Regulator Valve (PRV)
* Water Heater
* Irrigation Shutoff

## Gas System

* Gas Meter
* Main Gas Shutoff
* Appliance Gas Shutoffs

## Electrical System

* Main Electrical Disconnect
* Main Electrical Panel
* HVAC Disconnect

---

# Emergency Workflow Example

Water Leak Detected

1. Homeowner opens Barbarosa.
2. Selects Emergency.
3. Selects Water Leak.
4. Barbarosa displays:

   * Main Water Shutoff Location
   * Actual Photo of Shutoff
   * Instructions for Operation
5. Homeowner may:

   * Call Company Immediately
   * Request Callback
   * Upload Photos
   * Upload Video

# Service Request Integration

The Emergency Package will integrate with the Service Request System.

Example:

Water Leak
Location: Kitchen
Area: Ceiling
Severity: Active Leak

Photos Uploaded: Yes

Barbarosa may automatically reference:

* Previous Repipe Photos
* Previous Repair History
* Asset Records
* Equipment Photos

to assist technicians and dispatchers.

# Planning Notes

This feature is optional and should never be required during homeowner onboarding.

The homeowner should be encouraged to complete the Emergency Preparedness Package through education and demonstrated value rather than mandatory requirements.

This feature is considered one of the primary homeowner-facing value propositions of the Barbarosa platform.

BARBAROSA

170 - HOME HISTORY SYSTEM

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The Home History System (HomeFax) is the permanent
historical record of the property.

Every service, inspection, installation, replacement,
maintenance event, permit, document, and photo should
be capable of creating a Home History record.

Purpose:

* Preserve property history
* Increase homeowner awareness
* Improve future diagnostics
* Assist future buyers
* Assist inspectors
* Assist service technicians
* Create long-term value

==================================================
1000 - HOMEFAX PHILOSOPHY
=========================

Every home has a story.

Most homes lose their history because:

* Receipts are lost
* Photos disappear
* Owners move
* Contractors change
* Records are incomplete

Barbarosa preserves that history.

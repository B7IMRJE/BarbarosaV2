140 - ELECTRICAL MASTER INVENTORY

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The Electrical Master Inventory is the complete electrical asset database used by Barbarosa.

Every electrical component should be capable of storing:

* Health Score
* Status
* Photos
* Service History
* Installation Date
* Manufacturer
* Model Number
* Serial Number
* Warranty Information
* Documents
* Notes
* Manufacturer Recommendations

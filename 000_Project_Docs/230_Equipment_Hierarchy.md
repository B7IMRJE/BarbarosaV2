PURPOSE
==================================================

Organize all home assets into logical parent and child
relationships.

Assets should be grouped the way homeowners and
technicians naturally think about the home.

==================================================
1600 - FUTURE RULE
==================================================

Child assets should belong to a parent asset whenever
possible.

Example:

Water Heater
    └── Expansion Tank

Water Service
    └── PRV

Water Treatment
    └── Halo 5

This allows health scoring, service history,
documents, photos, and recommendations to be grouped
properly.

180 - DOCUMENTS AND WARRANTIES

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The Documents and Warranties System serves as the
permanent digital filing cabinet for the home.

Purpose:

* Organize important records
* Preserve warranties
* Preserve manuals
* Preserve permits
* Preserve invoices
* Preserve inspection reports
* Preserve photos and videos
* Support HomeFax records

==================================================

==================================================
1100 - ASSET LINKING
====================

Documents should be attached to assets whenever possible.

Example:

Water Heater

* Warranty
* Manual
* Invoice
* Permit
* Inspection Report
* Photos

Expansion Tank

* Warranty
* Invoice
* Photos

HVAC Condenser

* Warranty
* Manual
* Service Records
* Photos

Purpose:

Allow homeowners and technicians to find records
without searching through folders.

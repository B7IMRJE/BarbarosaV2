BARBAROSA

130 - HVAC MASTER INVENTORY

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The HVAC Master Inventory is the complete HVAC asset database used by Barbarosa.

Every HVAC component should be capable of storing:

* Health Score
* Status
* Photos
* Service History
* Installation Date
* Manufacturer
* Model Number
* Serial Number
* Warranty Information
* Documents
* Notes

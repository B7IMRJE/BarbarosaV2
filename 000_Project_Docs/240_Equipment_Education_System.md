240 - EQUIPMENT EDUCATION SYSTEM

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

Homeowners often do not understand the purpose,
function, maintenance requirements, or importance
of home equipment.

The Equipment Education System provides explanations
for each asset in homeowner-friendly language.

The goal is education, not sales.

==================================================
1000 - ABOUT THIS EQUIPMENT
===========================

Every equipment page should contain:

About This Equipment

Example:

Expansion Tank

An expansion tank helps absorb pressure created
when water expands during heating.

It helps reduce stress on plumbing components and
the water heater.

==================================================
1100 - WHY IT MATTERS
=====================

Every equipment page should explain:

Why It Matters

Example:

Pressure Regulator Valve

The PRV controls incoming water pressure.

High pressure can shorten the life of fixtures,
appliances, supply lines, and water heaters.

# 320 Home Dashboard

## Purpose

The Home Dashboard is the main landing page.

It provides a quick overview of the home's condition and access to all major areas of the app.

The dashboard must not contain fake data.

All displayed information must come from saved home data.

---

## Layout

Header

Welcome Home

Health Bar

## Home Health Status

Display:

Health Bar

Status Label

Examples:

Good
Needs Attention
Bad
Unknown

Status must come from saved data.

No hardcoded values.

If insufficient data exists:

Display:

"Not enough data yet"

---

## Health Breakdown

Display as tiles.

Initial systems:

Plumbing
HVAC
Electrical
Water Quality
Safety

Appliances
Gas
Exterior
Drains/Sewer
Documents

Two rows of five tiles.

Future systems may be added later.

Each tile is clickable.

Clicking a tile opens that system.

Example:

Plumbing
→ Plumbing Areas

HVAC
→ HVAC Areas

---

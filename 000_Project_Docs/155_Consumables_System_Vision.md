# Consumables System Vision

## Purpose

The Consumables System tracks items that wear out, get used up, or require periodic replacement.

The goal is to provide useful reminders without annoying the homeowner.

---

## Core Principle

One notification.

The homeowner chooses:

- Completed
- Snooze
- Remind Me Later
- Ignore

The system does not repeatedly nag the homeowner.

---

## Long Term Goal

Every piece of equipment can contain its own Consumables section.

Example:

Water Heater
- Anode Rod

Tankless
- Inlet Filter

Water Softener
- Salt

HVAC
- Air Filter

This allows Consumables to exist both independently and inside equipment records.

BARBAROSA

160 - HOME HEALTH ENGINE

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The Home Health Engine is responsible for evaluating
the overall condition of the home and presenting a
simple, easy-to-understand score to homeowners.

Purpose:

* Simplify home condition assessment
* Prioritize important issues
* Encourage preventative maintenance
* Improve homeowner awareness
* Create long-term HomeFax records

==================================================
1000 - HEALTH SCORE OVERVIEW
============================

Overall Home Health Score

Range:

0 - 100

Examples:

100 = Excellent

90 - 99 = Very Good

80 - 89 = Good

70 - 79 = Fair

60 - 69 = Poor

Below 60 = Immediate Attention Recommended

==================================================
1100 - SYSTEM SCORES
====================

Separate scores are calculated for:

* Plumbing
* HVAC
* Electrical
* Roofing
* Appliances
* Life Safety

Example:

Plumbing: 94

HVAC: 88

Electrical: 96

Roofing: 85

Life Safety: 100

Overall Home Health: 92

==================================================
1200 - EQUIPMENT SCORING
========================

Equipment affects Home Health.

Examples:

* Water Heater
* Expansion Tank
* PRV
* Water Softener
* HVAC Condenser
* Furnace
* Electrical Panel
* Generator

Equipment Factors:

* Age
* Condition
* Inspection Results
* Safety Issues
* Manufacturer Recommendations
* Service History

==================================================
1300 - CONSUMABLES
==================

Consumables do NOT directly reduce Home Health.

Examples:

* HVAC Filters
* Water Softener Salt
* UV Bulbs
* RO Filters
* Refrigerator Filters

Consumables affect:

Maintenance Status

not

Home Health Score

==================================================
1400 - SAFETY WEIGHTING
=======================

Safety items carry additional weight.

Examples:

* Active Water Leak
* Missing T&P Valve
* Missing Smoke Detector
* Missing CO Detector
* Unsafe Electrical Panel
* Gas Leak
* Missing Earthquake Straps
* Missing GFCI Protection
* Missing AFCI Protection

Safety items may significantly reduce scores.

==================================================
1500 - COSMETIC ITEMS
=====================

Cosmetic items have minimal impact.

Examples:

* Rusty Drain Pan
* Worn Faucet Finish
* Old Shower Trim
* Scratched Equipment Cover

These create recommendations but do not heavily impact scores.

==================================================
1600 - AGE CALCULATIONS
=======================

Example:

Water Heater

Expected Life:
12 Years

Formula:

100 ÷ 12

Each year reduces a percentage of health.

Additional reductions may apply for:

* Leaks
* Corrosion
* Failed Components
* Poor Maintenance

==================================================
1700 - SERVICE HISTORY BONUS
============================

Well-maintained equipment receives score benefits.

Examples:

Annual Water Heater Flushes

Documented Pressure Checks

Documented HVAC Service

Documented Generator Service

Purpose:

Reward preventative maintenance.

==================================================
1800 - INSPECTION CONFIDENCE
============================

Future Feature

Inspection Confidence Rating

High Confidence

* Recent inspection
* Recent photos
* Recent maintenance

Medium Confidence

* Some information missing

Low Confidence

* No recent records

==================================================
1900 - RECOMMENDATIONS ENGINE
=============================

Health Engine creates recommendations.

Examples:

Water Heater Health: 62

Recommendations:

* Flush Water Heater
* Replace Expansion Tank
* Schedule Inspection

HVAC Health: 70

Recommendations:

* Replace Filter
* Clean Condenser Coil
* Schedule HVAC Service

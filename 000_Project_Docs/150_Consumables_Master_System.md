BARBAROSA

150 - CONSUMABLES MASTER SYSTEM

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The Consumables System manages all recurring maintenance items within the home.

Consumables are items that are:

* Replaced
* Refilled
* Checked
* Cleaned
* Inspected

on a recurring basis.

Consumables do NOT use Health Scores.

Consumables use:

* Timers
* Status
* Reminders
* Completion Records
* Service History

==================================================
1000 - CONSUMABLE TYPES
=======================

TYPE A - REPLACE

Examples:

* HVAC Filter
* Media Filter
* HEPA Filter
* Refrigerator Filter
* RO Filter
* RO Membrane
* UV Bulb
* Smoke Detector Battery
* CO Detector Battery
* Humidifier Pad

TYPE B - CHECK

Examples:

* Water Softener Salt
* Expansion Tank Pressure
* Water Heater Flush
* Generator Oil Level
* Leak Sensor Battery
* Sump Pump Test

TYPE C - SERVICE

Examples:

* Water Heater Maintenance
* HVAC Maintenance
* Generator Service
* Water Softener Service
* RO System Service

==================================================
1100 - CONSUMABLE STATUS
========================

Green

* Good
* No Action Required

Yellow

* Due Soon

Red

* Overdue

Gray

* Reminder Disabled

==================================================
1200 - REMINDER PHILOSOPHY
==========================

Core Rule:

Barbarosa should help, not harass.

A reminder should be sent once.

The homeowner decides what happens next.

No repeated daily notifications.

==================================================
1300 - REMINDER ACTIONS
=======================

When a reminder appears:

Example:

HVAC Filter Due

Homeowner Options:

* Completed
* Snooze 7 Days
* Remind Me Later
* Request Service
* Disable Reminder

==================================================
1400 - COMPLETED ACTION
=======================

When homeowner selects:

Completed

Barbarosa automatically:

* Records completion date
* Creates Home History entry
* Calculates next due date
* Updates consumable status

Example:

Completed:
June 2, 2026

Next Due:
September 2, 2026

==================================================
1500 - SNOOZE ACTION
====================

Homeowner may choose:

* 7 Days
* 30 Days
* Custom Date

Barbarosa records snooze history.

==================================================
1600 - DISABLE REMINDER
=======================

Homeowner may disable reminders.

Status becomes:

Gray

Reminder Disabled

No future reminders will be sent unless re-enabled.

==================================================
1700 - REQUEST SERVICE
======================

Consumables can generate service requests.

Example:

HVAC Filter Due

Request Service

creates:

New Service Request

linked to the homeowner's provider.

==================================================
1800 - SMART SCHEDULING
=======================

Future Feature

Barbarosa learns maintenance patterns.

Example:

Water Softener Salt

Average Usage:

1 Bag Every 28 Days

Future reminders become more accurate.

==================================================
1900 - CONSUMABLE CONFIDENCE SCORE
==================================

Future Feature

Purpose:

Measure reliability of maintenance records.

Examples:

High Confidence

* Homeowner regularly records maintenance

Medium Confidence

* Occasional updates

Low Confidence

* No updates for long periods

==================================================
2000 - HOME HISTORY INTEGRATION
===============================

Every completed consumable action creates:

Home History Record

Examples:

2026
HVAC Filter Replaced

2026
Water Softener Salt Added

2026
Water Heater Flushed

These records become part of HomeFax.

==================================================
2100 - SERVICE OPPORTUNITIES
============================

Consumables may create recommendations.

Examples:

HVAC Filter overdue

Recommendation:
Schedule HVAC Inspection

Recommendation:

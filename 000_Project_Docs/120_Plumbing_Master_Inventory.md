BARBAROSA

120 - PLUMBING MASTER INVENTORY

VERSION 1

==================================================
PURPOSE
=======

The Plumbing Master Inventory is the complete plumbing asset database used by Barbarosa.

Every plumbing component should be capable of storing:

* Health Score
* Status
* Photos
* Service History
* Installation Date
* Manufacturer Information
* Model Information
* Serial Number
* Warranty Information
* Notes
* Service Records

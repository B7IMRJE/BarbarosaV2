120 - CONSUMABLES
=================

Purpose:
Track items requiring periodic maintenance or replacement.

Examples:

* HVAC Filters
* Water Softener Salt
* RO Filters
* UV Bulbs
* Water Heater Flush
* Water Heater Anode Rod
* Refrigerator Water Filter
* Humidifier Pad

Consumables use timers rather than health scores.

Each Consumable Contains:

* Status
* Last Completed Date
* Next Due Date
* Reminder Settings
* History

Color System:

Green = Good

Yellow = Due Soon

Red = Overdue

Gray = Reminder Disabled

==================================================
CONSUMABLE REMINDER PHILOSOPHY
==============================

Barbarosa should help, not harass.

Reminder Workflow:

1. Reminder appears.

2. Homeowner opens reminder.

3. Homeowner chooses:

* Completed
* Snooze 7 Days
* Remind Me Later
* Request Service
* Disable Reminder

No repeated daily notifications.

If homeowner selects Completed:

* Completion date recorded
* Next due date calculated
* History automatically updated
